Mask Guidance

We are excited to hear the governor's announcement lifting the mask mandate in Texas, and even more excited that numbers of Covid-19 cases in Texas are headed down and the number of vaccinations are going up. Even though this news has us hopeful and excited about the future, we will continue to respectfully ask all of our patients to wear a mask while being seen in order to protect those of us that haven't yet recieved the vaccine. We thank you and appreciate your help with this.
