About Mercy Wellness Clinic

Mercy Wellness Clinic is a Family Medical Practice dedicated to providing complete and compassionate care. We are based in Spring Branch, TX and work to fulfill the medical needs of the community. Mercy Wellness Clinic has been open since 2018, and was originally opened in Bulverde, TX but has since moved. We work closely with specialists and doctors in our community and all over San Antonio to provide the best medical care available.
